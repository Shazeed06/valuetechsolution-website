**Compiled:** 2026-05-11
**Scope:** Full keyword research, intent mapping, page assignment, 90-day action plan
**Markets:** India (primary), UAE, UK, US
**Audience:** Startup founders, SMB owners, marketing agencies hiring engineering

> ⚠️ Note on method: this strategy was synthesised from competitive-landscape
> analysis, search-intent patterns, and topical-authority modeling rather
> than live GSC / Keyword Planner pulls (those MCP integrations were
> offline). Search volumes and KD scores are **estimates** based on

## 1 · Keyword landscape overview

The site sits at the intersection of three competitive markets:

| Cluster | Competition | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **AI automation agency** | High (US/UK), Medium (IN) | Saturated globally, still winnable in IN/MENA |
| **n8n / GHL / Zapier services** | Low–Medium | Platform-specific keywords are under-served — biggest opportunity |
| **Next.js development agency** | High | Won't rank top-3 globally; can dominate "Next.js agency India" |
| **AI agents / RAG / automation consulting** | Medium | Rising trend, less mature SEO competition |

**Strategic posture:** stop trying to outrank "ai automation agency" head terms head-on. Win by:
1. **Platform-specific authority** — own "n8n agency", "GoHighLevel agency", "Zapier consultant"
2. **Geographic long-tail** — own "AI automation agency Bangalore / Dubai / London"
3. **Comparison / vs content** — capture commercial-investigation traffic
4. **Industry × service** — "AI automation for SaaS", "Zapier setup for ecommerce"

## 11 · Things to AVOID (real SEO mistakes)

- ❌ **Keyword stuffing** — title repetition, density >4%
- ❌ **Thin location pages** without unique content
- ❌ **AI-generated blog posts** without human edit pass (Google's helpful-content update penalises)
- ❌ **Duplicate H1s** across pages (each page = unique keyword phrase)
- ❌ **Generic anchor text** ("click here", "read more") on internal links
- ❌ **Cloaking, hidden text, doorway pages** — site is too clean to risk this
- ❌ **Buying low-quality backlinks** — disavows take months
